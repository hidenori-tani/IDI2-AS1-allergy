**Reason for withdrawal**

When I accepted the OUP Transfer Desk offer on 2026-05-19, I prepared the BFG submission package under the assumption that BFG offered a subscription publication route, and the cover letter and title page indicated this route accordingly. After submission, I have become aware that *Briefings in Functional Genomics* moved to a **fully Open Access model on 1 January 2025**, under which all accepted manuscripts are subject to an APC charge.

As declared in the manuscript Funding statement, this work received **no specific funding**, and as a single-author researcher I am not in a position to meet the APC at the standard OUP OA rate. The transition to OA-only publication materially changes the conditions under which I would otherwise have proceeded.

I therefore respectfully ask that the manuscript be **withdrawn from consideration at this stage**, before any editorial decision or peer review is initiated, so that I may submit it to a venue compatible with my funding constraints.

I am grateful to the OUP Transfer Desk for facilitating the transfer, and I appreciate the editorial office's prompt action on this withdrawal request. Please confirm withdrawal in writing when complete.
